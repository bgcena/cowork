Mumbai Monsoon Big Update: Mumbai, Thane, Raigad, Ratnagiri, Nagpur, Satara, Sangli likely to receive rain — IMD issues alert

As the intensity of rainfall increased in the suburbs from Sunday night, Santacruz recorded 103.1 mm of rainfall till 8:30 am on Monday morning

🚨 IMD Alert Active Orange alert for Mumbai, Thane, Raigad, Ratnagiri. Yellow alert issued for 23 districts across Maharashtra.

Mumbai: What can be termed as a piece of good news for Maharashtra, the monsoon is likely to arrive in some districts of the state today. Several places have already received satisfactory rainfall. The next few hours are expected to be particularly important for the Konkan region, where the intensity of rainfall is likely to increase further. On June 29, the monsoon intensified in most parts of the state.

The showers have brought much-needed relief and joy across the Konkan belt. An Orange Alert has been issued for Wednesday for the districts of Mumbai, Thane, Raigad, and Ratnagiri. The monsoon winds became active once again in Mumbai from Sunday night.

Mumbai Rains: Key Rainfall Figures

  • Santacruz recorded 103.1 mm of rainfall till 8:30 am on Monday morning.
  • Colaba received 30.4 mm during the same period.
  • Mulund municipal school recorded 210 mm of rainfall.
  • Gavanpada Fire Brigade Centre received 167 mm of rain.
  • Versova also recorded 167 mm, Goregaon registered 129 mm.

According to the weather department, the Konkan region is expected to receive the heaviest rainfall. Districts such as Palghar, Thane, and Mumbai are likely to witness heavy rain. There is a possibility of heavy to very heavy rainfall in Ratnagiri and Sindhudurg districts, for which an orange alert has been issued.

Western Maharashtra

Heavy rains will continue in western Maharashtra. Light to moderate rains with thundershowers and gusty winds are likely in Pune and Satara districts. Heavy rains are more likely in the Pune Ghatmatha area. There is a yellow alert for heavy rains in Kolhapur and Satara Ghatmatha areas. Light to moderate rains with thundershowers are likely in Kolhapur, Sangli and Solapur districts.

North Maharashtra

A yellow alert for light to moderate rainfall accompanied by thundershowers in Jalgaon, Nashik, Nashik Ghatmatha, Dhule and Ahilyanagar districts. Light to moderate rainfall accompanied by gusty winds is also likely in Nandurbar district.

Marathwada

A yellow alert has been issued for Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar, Jalna, Parbhani, Beed, Hingoli and Nanded districts. Light to moderate monsoon with gusty winds is expected in Latur and Dharashiv.

Vidarbha

Dry weather is expected in Akola, Buldhana and Washim districts. Heavy rains with gusty winds are expected in Amravati. The monsoon will arrive in Gondia, Nagpur, Wardha, Bhandara, Chandrapur, Gadchiroli and Yavatmal with thundershowers and gusty winds.
